About us

Passion for food has driven me to every nook and corner of the world. From becoming the youngest executive chef at a five star hotel, to hosting my own cooking shows, it is my love for cooking that has got this far. Through this website, I intend to take you on a culinary journey, exploring new places, cultures and flavours. Let’s engage, exchange and evolve our skills and taste buds.
